Info

This website is designed for users/students to be able to make canteen orders ahead of time, and pay at the counter. This should increase the efficiency of the canteen reducing queue times and hopefully slowing down COVID transmision.

To use the site simply make an account by clicking "Create account", then login and navigate to the order page, then select the items you want in your order, set a date for your order and click submit. Now your order is in the system, all that is now required is to turn up on the date, pay, and recive your items.
